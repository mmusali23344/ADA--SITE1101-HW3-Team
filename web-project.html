Preparation

Firstly, our team leader (Murad) worked on GitHub by himself so he could remember all GitHub transactions, and he researched how to work together in one team by implementing Git at the same time. After Murad finished all his tests, he explained them to the other team members, and they also did their testing on GitHub. Then we began to write our own tasks given by our team leader. The team leader helped other teammates as he had experience in developing websites. Especially with his help, we could somehow understand all positions, types of displays, margins, paddings, etc. For better understanding, we also collaborated either online or in person.

Practice

Also, for understanding many concepts, we practiced a lot by writing simple pages so that we could implement them into our project. This really worked, so we could write our code correctly. Sometimes we encountered some troubles with sizes, but our team leader helped with the solutions, which made it easier to manage our pages.

Implementation

After we were prepared enough, we began to write our tasks. Three of us created pages with simple designs and CSS, wrote the report, and created the team logo, while our team leader created carcass of the project(sidebar and navigation) and enhanced the design using the knowledge he had gained previously. Also, he added some transitions and animations to our website, making it look better.
